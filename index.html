The
Social Learning
Summit 2015

April 18th & 19th, 2015 // American University

The Social Learning Summit presented by American University's Social Media Club is a two-day jam-packed conference of industry-focused panel discussions and keynotes designed to explore the unique impact of social media on all of our lives.

Panel Topics

Attendee Info

From social media newbies to mavens, all are invited to participate in the two-day conference. No matter if you are a hashtag pro or a hashtag novice, we guarantee that anyone who attends #SLS15 will learn fresh new strategies and tactics from some of the savviest brands out there.

What to bring:
  • Proof of registration (digital or physical)
  • Your favorite device tweeting with us
  • Your appetite for hashtags, and for freebies
Location and Directions:

American Unviersity
4400 Massachusetts Avenue NW
Washington, D.C. 20016

The Friday event is located in the new McKinley Building, accessible from the quad. All Saturday events are located in the School of International Service. Free underground parking is available below this building from Wisconson Avenue. Free shuttle service is also available from the Tenleytown Metro Station.
